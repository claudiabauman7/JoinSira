Why Sira exists

The fishing rod and the ocean

I sat in a village in Ghana and watched some of the most capable, driven, intelligent people I had ever met describe exactly what they needed and exactly where they wanted to go. The only wall between them and the rest of the world was the passport they were born holding.

When I returned to Spain and tried to help a friend get a simple tourist visa, the process was nearly impossible. Information was scattered, lawyers were expensive, and the system was built for people who already had access.

"You cannot learn to fish in the desert. Some people know exactly what they would do if only someone handed them the fishing rod and showed them the way to the ocean."

JoinSira exists to build that map — the data, the community, and the human bridge that connects talent to opportunity, regardless of the passport you hold.
